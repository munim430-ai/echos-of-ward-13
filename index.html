The Story

You wake on a cold metal table with no memory. A note pinned to your chest reads: "The Dark hears your heartbeat. Find the three keys before it finds you." Ward 13 is waiting. So is The Patient—a 7-foot-tall entity with no eyes that hunts by sound alone. Your flashlight is dying. Your fear is rising. And something in the darkness is listening to every breath you take.
